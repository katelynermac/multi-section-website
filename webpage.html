Personal Reflection

As a student in Tangub City Global College who's known as to their "Una ang Ginoo", I sometimes face challenges that leave me feeling uncertain and doubt in everything.
But i know every challenges that i face, has a purpose and that is to have faith in him. I may not always understand why certain problems comes my way, but I believe
God uses these moments to strengthen me.

Faith teaches me to keep going, to pray, and to stay hopeful even when things don't go according to my plan. Each day, I try to place my worries in God's hands and focus
on doing my best — believing He will guide my steps. Through my faith in Him, I feel more grounded, more secure, and more hopeful to what will come in the future.

"For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord, “plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you a future and a hope."

- Jeremiah 29:11

Community

At Tangub City Global College, We students are encouraged to actively participate in initiatives that support and uplift our community. Through outreach programs, clean-up drives, donation campaigns, and youth mentoring, We students are not just the light of the world of our school, We are students that brings the light of hope who will give meaningful difference in the lives of others and to the community.

Inspire

This portfolio is a collection of the experiences, lessons, and inspirations that helped shape me for being who i am now.
